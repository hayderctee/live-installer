Contributing to the development of Uruk Project

you can help the project In many ways! Here are some:


- Using Uruk GNU/Linux, and sharing it with your friends and family.
- Reporting issues and bugs that you find.
- Suggesting improvements.
- Helping in the free forums.
- Writing and translating documentations or applications.
- Designing .
- If you have any experience to developing software for Uruk GNU/Linux.


So as you see, anyone would have the skills to help in some way, but if you lack the time you can still contribute by spreading Uruk GNU/Linux through social networks, publications and free software events.
